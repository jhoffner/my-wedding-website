Other Options

AirBnb

For those of you traveling in groups, you may find the best deal on AirBnb. We vagabonded for 6 months using AirBnb a few years ago and can attest to how well it works.

Air

Santa Barbara (SBA)

If you can swing it SBA will be the best airport to fly into. It is about 50 minutes away from Ojai. For those flying in on Thursday night, we may be able to arrange someone to pick you up from the airport. Please reach out to us and we will see if we can accomodate you.

Los Angeles (LAX)

LAX is about 90 minutes away from Ojai without traffic. Its LA, there is usually traffic, so unless you are flying in late at night you will probably want to budget about 2 hours of driving time.

LAX will be your cheapest option. For family flying from Baltimore, Jennifer’s father will be able to organize a group discount that as of now is $377 round-trip for 5 or more people. Spirit airlines may also be an ever more cost efficient option. If you consider flying Spirit please take note of why its costs are lower and make sure you will not incure additional costs (such as carry on baggage).

Group travel into LAX is highly recommended as you will be able to save money by carpooling.

Parking

Estate Parking

The estate has limited parking and will likely be mostly taken by the guests who will be sleeping at the estate. For this reason we will need to carpool whenever possible when traveling to and from hotels.

There will be no parking allowed on the street outside of the estate. We will be ticketed if you do so. However there should be parking available down the street from the estate and would only require a short walk.

Church Parking

Overnight parking is made available @ the Ojai Presbyterian Church located at 304 N. Foothill Rd. The church is close to the estate, but not within a comfortable walking distance.

The church will be the primary pickup spot for shuttle service on Saturday.

Shuttles

A limited shuttle service will be available to guests on the day of the wedding. We have the shuttle for 3 hours and will have specific windows in which it will take guests to and from the venue.

Shuttle Pickup Time & Location

The shuttle will begin pickup at 4:30 at the Capri Hotel. For guests staying at the hotel we recommend you begin waiting outside 10 minutes early. It will then proceed to the Ojai Presbyterian Church for pickup. We only have the shuttle for an hour (from 4:30 to 5:30) for pickup so please be at your pickup location on time.

Shuttle Return

The shuttle service will begin its return service at 10 PM and run for 2 hours. It will drop you off at either the church or the Capri Hotel.

Uber

Uber X is available in Ojai and will be a great way to get around without having to worry about parking, shuttle times or drunk driving. The hotels are very close to the property so with enough coordination from other guests (ride sharing) the costs should be minimal.
